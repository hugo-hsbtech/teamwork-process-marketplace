# Submitter brief — Self-service seat management

Recorded by: Ana Costa (Customer Success), 2026-03-12, from a renewal call with
Banco Meridional.

## The pain
Enterprise admins cannot manage their own user seats. Every add/remove/reassign is
a support ticket with a 1–3 business-day turnaround. During onboarding waves admins
file 20–30 tickets in a week and wait days for each, so new hires sit without access.

## Who feels it
- Enterprise admins at Banco Meridional (the direct requesters).
- New hires blocked from access while a ticket is pending.
- Our support team: ~40% of Banco Meridional's tickets last quarter were seat changes.

## Why now
Contract renewal in 90 days; CS flagged the manual seat process as a churn risk that
was raised explicitly on the renewal-agenda call.
